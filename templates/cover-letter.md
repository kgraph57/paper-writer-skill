# Cover Letter Template

## Format

```
[Date]

[Editor Name]
Editor-in-Chief
[Journal Name]

Dear [Editor Name / Dear Editor],

We would like to submit our manuscript entitled "{TITLE}" for consideration for publication in {Journal Name}.

[Paragraph 1: What the study is about and why it matters]
In this {study design}, we investigated {research question} in {population}. {Brief description of key methods}.

[Paragraph 2: Key findings]
Our findings demonstrate that {main result}. {One sentence on clinical significance}.

[Paragraph 3: Why this journal]
We believe this manuscript is appropriate for {Journal Name} because {reason: scope, readership, related published articles}.

[Paragraph 4: Declarations]
This manuscript has not been published and is not under consideration for publication elsewhere. All authors have read and approved the manuscript. {Ethics statement if required}. {Conflicts of interest statement}. {Funding statement}.

We look forward to hearing from you.

Sincerely,

[Corresponding Author Name]
[Affiliation]
[Email]
[ORCID]
```

## Tips

- Keep it under 1 page
- Address the editor by name if possible (check journal website)
- Mention why YOUR paper fits THIS journal specifically
- Include required declarations (varies by journal)
- Suggest reviewers if the journal requests it (add a section)
- Mention excluded reviewers if applicable
