# Acknowledgments Template

> Acknowledge contributions that do not meet authorship criteria (ICMJE).

---

## Template

```
ACKNOWLEDGMENTS

[The authors thank / We are grateful to] [Name(s)] ([Affiliation]) for
[specific contribution — e.g., statistical consultation, technical assistance,
data collection, manuscript editing, administrative support].

[We thank the patients/participants who contributed to this study.]

[We acknowledge [Organization] for providing access to [database/equipment/facility].]

[AI tools]: [Name of AI tool (e.g., ChatGPT-4, Claude, Grammarly)] was used for
[specific task — e.g., language editing, literature search assistance, code review].
The authors take full responsibility for the content and accuracy of the manuscript.

[Medical writing]: Medical writing support was provided by [Name] ([Company]),
funded by [Funder]. [Name] did not influence the scientific content.
```

## Rules
- Everyone acknowledged should have given **written permission**
- ICMJE: persons who do not meet ALL 4 authorship criteria belong in Acknowledgments
- If AI tools were used for writing, some journals require disclosure HERE (not just in Methods)
- Medical writers and statistical consultants should be acknowledged
- Do NOT list authors in Acknowledgments (they are in the author list)
