# Conflict of Interest (COI) Detailed Guide

> Accurate COI disclosure is legally and ethically required. Incomplete disclosure can lead to retraction, career damage, and loss of public trust. ICMJE requires ALL authors to complete individual COI forms.

---

## Categories of Conflicts

### 1. Financial Conflicts

| Category | Examples | Threshold |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| **Research funding** | Grants, contracts from industry | Any amount (disclose all) |
| **Employment** | Current/recent employment by a company with interest | Any |
| **Consulting fees** | Advisory board, expert testimony | Often >$5,000/year triggers disclosure |
| **Honoraria** | Speaker fees, lecture fees | Often >$5,000/year |
| **Stock/equity** | Ownership, options in relevant companies | Any |
| **Patents/royalties** | IP related to the research topic | Any |
| **Travel/accommodation** | Industry-sponsored travel | Any |
| **Gifts** | Equipment, materials from industry | Value varies by institution |

### 2. Non-Financial (Personal) Conflicts

| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| **Personal relationships** | Family member employed by interested party |
| **Academic competition** | Rival research group, disputed priority |
| **Intellectual bias** | Strong advocacy for a particular theory |
| **Professional affiliations** | Board membership in advocacy groups |
| **Political/religious beliefs** | When relevant to the research topic |

### 3. Institutional Conflicts

| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| **Institutional funding** | University receiving major grant from company |
| **Industry partnerships** | Joint ventures, licensing agreements |
| **Departmental relationships** | Research center named after donor |
| **Revenue generation** | Institution holding patents on studied intervention |

## ICMJE COI Form

### Current Form
- **ICMJE Uniform Disclosure Form** (updated 2024)
- Available at: https://www.icmje.org/disclosure-of-interest/
- Required: ONE form per author
- Format: Online fillable PDF or web form
- Covers: Previous 36 months of potential conflicts

### Form Sections

1. **Related financial activities outside the submitted work**
   - Grants or contracts (entity, amount optional)
   - Consulting fees
   - Payment or honoraria
   - Payment for expert testimony
   - Support for attending meetings
   - Patents planned, issued, or pending
   - Participation on advisory board
   - Leadership or fiduciary role
   - Stock or stock options
   - Receipt of equipment, materials, drugs, medical devices
   - Other financial or non-financial interests

2. **Relevant financial activities related to the submitted work**
   - Funding for the research itself

3. **Intellectual property**
   - Patents, copyrights, royalties

4. **Other relationships or activities**
   - Anything else readers should know

## How to Write the COI Statement

### No Conflicts

```
CONFLICT OF INTEREST STATEMENT

The authors declare no conflicts of interest relevant to this article.
```

or

```
The authors have no financial or non-financial interests to disclose.
```

### With Conflicts (Template)

```
CONFLICT OF INTEREST STATEMENT

[Author Name] reports grants from [Company] (for the submitted work);
consulting fees from [Company]; and honoraria for lectures from [Company]
(outside the submitted work). [Author 2] holds stock in [Company].
All other authors declare no conflicts of interest.
```

### Detailed Format (Annals of Internal Medicine style)

```
Disclosures: Authors have disclosed no conflicts of interest. Forms can
be viewed at www.acponline.org/authors/icmje/ConflictOfInterestForms.do?
msNum=M22-XXXX.
```

## CRediT (Contributor Roles Taxonomy)

NISO Z39.104-2022 standard. Increasingly required by journals.

| Role | Description |
|------|------------|
| **Conceptualization** | Ideas; formulation of research goals |
| **Data curation** | Management and annotation of data |
| **Formal analysis** | Statistical or mathematical analysis |
| **Funding acquisition** | Financial support for the project |
| **Investigation** | Conducting research and data collection |
| **Methodology** | Development of methodology |
| **Project administration** | Management and coordination |
| **Resources** | Materials, reagents, patients, samples, computing |
| **Software** | Programming, implementation |
| **Supervision** | Oversight for the research activity |
| **Validation** | Verification of results |
| **Visualization** | Data presentation, figures |
| **Writing – original draft** | Initial writing |
| **Writing – review & editing** | Critical review and revision |

### How to Report CRediT

```
AUTHOR CONTRIBUTIONS

Conceptualization: A.B., C.D.; Methodology: A.B., E.F.; Formal Analysis:
E.F.; Investigation: A.B., G.H.; Writing – Original Draft: A.B.;
Writing – Review & Editing: all authors; Supervision: C.D.;
Funding Acquisition: C.D.
```

## ORCID

### What It Is
- Open Researcher and Contributor ID
- 16-digit persistent digital identifier
- Format: 0000-000X-XXXX-XXXX
- Free registration at https://orcid.org

### Why It Matters
- ICMJE recommends ORCID for all authors (required by some journals since 2024)
- Disambiguates authors with similar names
- Links all publications to one profile
- Required by many funders

### Journal Requirements

| Journal/Publisher | ORCID Required? |
|------------------|----------------|
| JAMA Network | Corresponding author required |
| Nature/Springer | All authors encouraged |
| PLOS | All authors required |
| Elsevier | Corresponding author required |
| Wiley | Corresponding author encouraged |
| BMJ | All authors encouraged |

## 日本語

### 利益相反（COI）開示

- 日本医学会のCOI開示基準を参照
- 過去3年間の利益相反を開示
- 各学会のCOI自己申告書を使用
- 金銭的COI：企業からの研究費（100万円以上）、顧問料（100万円以上）、株式保有（利益100万円以上 or 5%以上）
- ICMJE形式のCOI formは英文ジャーナル投稿時に必要
- CRediTは日本語ジャーナルでも採用が増加中

### 日本語COI記載テンプレート

```
利益相反

[著者名]は[企業名]から研究助成金を受けている。[著者名]は[企業名]の
顧問を務めている。その他の著者は申告すべき利益相反はない。
```

```
利益相反：本研究に関連する利益相反はない。
```
