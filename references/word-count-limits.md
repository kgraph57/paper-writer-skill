## Word Count Tips

### What counts toward word limits?
- **Included**: Main text body (Introduction through Discussion/Conclusion)
- **Usually excluded**: Title page, abstract, references, tables, figure legends, acknowledgments
- **Check**: Some journals include tables and figure legends in word count
- **Headers**: Section headings may or may not count

### How to count
```
# In Word: Review → Word Count (check "Include textboxes, footnotes and endnotes")
# In LaTeX: texcount manuscript.tex
# Quick estimate: references ≈ 30 words each
```

### If over the limit
1. Cut Discussion first (usually longest and most cuttable)
2. Move data to Supplementary Material
3. Combine similar Tables
4. Remove redundancies between Results text and Tables
5. Shorten Introduction (aim for 3 tight paragraphs)
6. Consider a different article type (Letter instead of Original Article)
